About FinCred Credit Scores

FinCred® is an app created to helping our users improve their financial wellbeing. The application is used for users to check their credit worthiness. • Users provide their credit history and the information is used to estimate their credit score. • The credit score ranges between 300-850 and graded with highest score attaining an excellent remark and the least scores attaining an adverse remark. The higher the score, the better a borrower looks to potential lenders. • FinCred also provides recommendations to users on financial institutions with affordable interest rates to approach based on the credit score outcome.
